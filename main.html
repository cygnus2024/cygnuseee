About the Cygnus


CYGNUS is a National level technical symposium that is conducted every year by the Department of ELECTRICAL AND ELECTRONICS ENGINEERING. This symposium serves as a platform for discussing upcoming trends, exploring the latest technological advancements, and sharing innovative research. Imagine a world where technical prowess meets boundless fun. Here are 8 events spanning various domains that infuse technology with excitement and joy. All these events will feature guest speakers and experts who will share their knowledge and insights with the attendees. This symposium plays a crucial role in bridging the academic and industrial gap which contributes to the advancement of innovation and technology in ELECTRICAL AND ELECTRONICS ENGINEERING.

About Crescent

Established in 1984, B.S. Abdur Rahman Crescent Institute of Science and Technology, situated in Chennai near Tambaram, is a distinguished institution with a 38-year legacy of academic excellence. Our intellectual hub challenges conventional thinking, fostering a passion for redefining learning. With top- notch education, a green campus, and vibrant student life, we provide a conducive environment for holistic development. Our diverse educational programs and student development clubs create a community that nurtures personal and professional growth, making our institution a lifelong home.




General Guidlines

  • Free registration for the entire event except Food Feast
  • Only registered participants will be allowed to attend the event , on spot registrations will not be entertained.
  • It is mandatory for the participants to provide their college ID at the help desk for verification.
  • Proper dress code should be followed by the participants.
  • A participant is not restricted to participation in any number of events as long as the event timings do not clash.
